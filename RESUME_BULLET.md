# Résumé bullets — Indirect Tax Compliance & Analytics (SQL)

Pick the version that fits your résumé's space. All are quantified and tied to
the actual project results.

## Primary (1–2 lines, recommended)

- **Built an indirect (sales & use) tax compliance analytics database in SQL
  (SQLite)** — designed an 8-table schema with effective-dated tax rates and
  authored 20+ analytical queries (CTEs, window functions) that automated tax
  determination, exemption-certificate validation, post-*Wayfair* economic-nexus
  tracking, and collected-vs-remitted reconciliation, surfacing **$2,688 in
  unsupported-exemption tax exposure** and **$285 in unremitted tax** across 5
  state jurisdictions.
